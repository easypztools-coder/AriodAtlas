- Elongated, narrow leaves with deep, flame-like lobing and internal fenestrations resembling ribs

- Slender green petioles without wings or sheath wrapping
- Thick, stubby aerial roots emerging from the nodes
- Graceful, upright climbing growth habit requiring vertical support

Elongated, narrow, flame-like leaves with dramatic, deep internal fenestrations and thick lobes resembling ribs

Smooth, glossy, and firm (coriaceous) leaf substance

Petiole:
Green, relatively slender, cylindrical, and sturdy

Climbing hemiepiphyte with a graceful, sculptural habit, producing thick aerial roots from nodes
